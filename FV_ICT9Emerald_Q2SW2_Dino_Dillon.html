CAT BREEDS
IMAGE BREED DESCRIPTION
BRITISH SHORTHAIR The British Shorthair is a medium- to large-sized cat with a great deal of strength. She has thick boning and a lot of muscle. She appears to be heavy and rounded. A British Shorthair cat makes a wonderful pet friend. She is calm and laid-back. The British cat is a fiercely devoted, adoring creature who will cling to every member of her household.
MAINE COON The Maine Coon is a large cat with an athletic build that matches their stunning size. They have broad, pointy ears held erect and wide, and they look intelligent.
BOMBAY CAT Although stocky and fairly compact, this breed has thick boning and is quite muscular. The entire Bombay is rounded. The roundness of the head extends to the tips of the ears, the eyes, the chin, and even the feet. Younger Bombays are energetic, inquisitive cats who rapidly adjust to change. They enjoy observing their surroundings at any age, and their favorite spot is frequently a window that allows them to do so. These cats are incredibly affectionate and occasionally try to get their parents to spend time with them.
